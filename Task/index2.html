About Us:

FLUX is an official club at Samrat Ashok Technological Institute focused on enhancing students' technical knowledge and promoting a tech-friendly environment. It primarily specialises in robotics and has organised various technical events.

VISION:

Our vision is to provide students of the institute a platform that nurtures a culture of constant learning, collaboration, imaginative thinking, and sparks creativity, empowering members to boldly explore, experiment, and reach new heights in various technical fields. Our club envisions a dynamic environment where students from various disciplines come together to exchange ideas, engage in hands-on learning, and embark on exciting projects that push the boundaries of technology. Through our club's initiatives, workshops, and events, our intention is to build a culture that embraces curiosity, adept problem-solving, and discerning critical thinking. We strive to establish a supportive and collaborative space that promotes knowledge sharing, networking, and mentorship among all members.

MISSION:

The mission of our college technical club is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the field of technology. We are dedicated to providing our members with a platform to enhance their technical skills, foster innovation, and cultivate a passion for lifelong learning and along with that to provide a platform that empowers students to participate in experiential learning, collaborate on meaningful projects, and access growth opportunities for their professional journey.

RECRUITMENT PROCEDURE

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES:

  • Students can apply for different domains of the club depending upon their skills, such as, Content Writing, Graphic Designing, Video Editing, Management, Speaker, Technical Team.
  • Students will be interviewed by a panel of Final year members of the club which will include, President, Vice President, Student Coordinator as well as mandatory presence of the faculty coordinator/faculty co-coordinator.
  • Students will be first evaluated on the basis of skills they have applied for and then will be interviewed by the panel, who will evaluate students on the basis of their communication skills, the skills they have applied for, learning potential, Team work & Co-ordination, Time management.
  • A person can join ONLY 3 Student bodies including flux (flux and any 2 student bodies). These student bodies can be NCC/NSS, any other official/unofficial club of the college. If any member wants to join any 4th club, he/she shall have to take written permission from the president of the Club. Permission will be granted only on the basis of past contribution of the member.

As a result, attend auditions only after carefully examining your membership in several clubs.

  • Register -

    Do register yourself by filling all the details in the google form with uttermost sincerity. Be extra mindful of choosing your domain of expertise to contribute and you wish to apply for within the club, as you will be assigned tasks on that basis.

  • Submission –

    After filling the registration form, you will be assigned certain tasks on the basis of the domain you apply for via mail. Submit the allotted task within the deadline, otherwise it won’t be considered. Deadline for submission of tasks will be told through mails only.

  • Personal Interview –

    On the basis of task submission, shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview round, that is, final round. The date and time slot for the Interview will be mailed as well as informed to you via call too.
